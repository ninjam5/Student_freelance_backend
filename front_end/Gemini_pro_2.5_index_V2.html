Our Story Since 1990

Founded in 1990, Sha8alny began as a small startup with a revolutionary vision: to connect talented freelancers with businesses in need of their expertise.

Over three decades, we've facilitated millions of successful projects, from small startups to Fortune 500 companies. Our platform has grown to serve over 50 million freelancers and 5 million businesses worldwide.

Today, Sha8alny continues to innovate, offering cutting-edge tools, secure payment systems, and AI-powered matching to help freelancers and clients find their perfect match.
